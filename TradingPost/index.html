🎁 Rewards Trading Post – Points Redemption Policy

  1. Earning Points
    Rewards Points are earned on the total purchase price, including sales tax and shipping. For every $1 spent, you earn 100 points. Put simply: every $10 spent earns you $1 in Rewards Points.
  2. Point Value
    Each point is worth $0.10 toward future purchases. Your points balance is automatically updated after each completed order.
  3. Redemption at Checkout
    Points can be applied directly during checkout. You may only redeem up to the amount available in your balance. The checkout field will automatically restrict entry to your current balance.
  4. Partial Redemptions
    You may choose to redeem all or part of your available points. Any unused points remain in your account for future orders.
  5. Expiration
    Points do not expire as long as your account remains active. Inactive accounts (no purchases for 18 months) may forfeit unused points.
  6. Non‑Transferable
    Rewards Points are tied to your account and cannot be transferred, exchanged for cash, or gifted.
  7. Program Updates
    Picante Pequin Salsa Co. reserves the right to adjust earning rates, redemption values, or program rules. Any changes will be posted here in advance.
